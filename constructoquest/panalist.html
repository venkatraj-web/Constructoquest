Ravi Murthy

Financial Advisor at AGF Investment

About Me

Mr Ravi Murthy is a qualified Chartered Accountant from ICAI, Master Financial Controller from American Academy of Financial Management and Certified Financial Consultant from Institute of Financial Consultants USA & Canada.

With over 40 years of experience in the service, manufacturing and contracting industries, Mr. Murthy has held various senior Finance positions in India & UAE before joining Arabtec Construction in 1996 as a Chief Finance Officer where he worked until 2019. He was also Group Chief Finance Officer of Arabtec Holding during the interim period for 2 years, credited with successful restructuring of the group amongst many other achievements.

He was a key team member involved in the strategic planning for the transition of Arabtec from a Private Company to a Public Listed Company on the Dubai Financial Market (DFM) in 2004, being the first Construction firm to become publicly listed in the region.

Mr. Murthy was listed in the “Top 50 Indian Executives in the Arab World” by Forbes M.E. during 2017 & 2018 for his achievements.
